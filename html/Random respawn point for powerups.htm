This is easy to do. And the powerups need not even be predefined in the map either.
In fact just look at how the give cmd works.
It spawns the requested item right at your current location. All you need to do is, after the worldspawn has spawned, spawn a few powerup spawners that randomly (or not so randomly) spawn powerups with random origins. Just make sure that the random point is not in a solid brush. You'll also need to set the spawnflags properly for the powerups so that they will fall (if that is what you want them to do)
